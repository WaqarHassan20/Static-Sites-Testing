Transportation Options

The city center is easily accessible by metro, bus, taxi, and ride-sharing services. The Downtown Metro Station is located at the heart of the entertainment district, with connections to all major attractions. Buses run every 10-15 minutes during peak hours.
Take the Red Line metro to Museum Plaza Station. From there, it's a 5-minute walk to all major museums. Alternatively, the Museum Shuttle Bus (Route 42) stops at every museum entrance. Parking is available at the Museum Garage for $12/day.
Yes! Multiple parking garages are located throughout the city. Downtown Garage ($15/day), Museum Parking ($12/day), and Entertainment District Parking ($18/day). Street parking is also available with metered rates of $3/hour. We recommend arriving early on weekends.
